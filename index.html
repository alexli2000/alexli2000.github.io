Demestify

Project Description

This was an iPad application that my team and I created for the Dementia Hack. We focused on the patients that suffered from Dementia and had three goals.

The first was to stimulate them on a daily basis. We did this through giving them a virtual pet that they were in charge of taking care of. It would need to go the bathroom, have fun, be fed, and get sick (very similar to a 'Tamagotchi'). The user would also be able to buy clothes and accessories to customize it.

Our second goal was to help the patient suffering from Dementia remember important, but tiny, pieces of their life. This is where the games come in. In order to buy food, medicine, clothes, etc. for their pet, the user has to complete games. We managed to program demos of two games. The first is one where they rearrange given letters to form a word matching a description. For example, 'The country I visited in 1998' would be 'Mexico'. The second game involved solving a puzzle that was made of a photo they uploaded. This was meant to help them remember the experiences and people that matter most to them.

The last part of the app was a way for the caregiver to monitor the progress of the user. They would have their own panel that they could monitor their usage and the time required to solve certain games. It was also a sort of admin panel that allowed them to uplod new words and phots to further customize the games that the user was interacting with.

Overall, it was a great team experience and I learned a lot about getting things done on a strict, short timeline. Also, it was amazing to try and create an a piece of software that people could derive a benefit from.
